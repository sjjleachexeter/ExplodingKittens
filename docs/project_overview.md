# Project Overview
Food has a story that is rarely told. This website will help bring it to light.

## Objectives
- Improve awareness of the cost and environmental impact of making certain food purchases.
- Educate consumers on product traceability and sustainability.
#### Considerations: Money, Environment, Season/quality.

## Target Users
First year University students who are having to purchase and cook for themselves for the first time.
- Students may be concerned with the price of foods at certain times of the year.
- Students may be concerned with the welfare of animal products they buy, or with their environmental impact.

-  QR/Barcode scanner
