Missions

Easy Missions

1. First Scan

Scan any product and view its full product passport .

2. Multi-Stage

Scan a product that has at least 3 different stages.

3. Main Ingredient Origin

Medium Missions

4. Evidence Match

Scan products with claims such as 'locally sourced ' 'Fair Trade' , 'organic'.

5. Missing Evidence

Scan an item and identify what extra evidence is needed to support a claim.

Hard Missions

7. Weak Evidence

Scan a product where a major claim is supported by only one weak document.

8. Wrong Evidence Type

Scan a product with the wrong type of evidence.

Relative Value Challenges

Value Contribution

Scan a chocolate bar and analyze its product passport to identify which stage—from raw cocoa farming to retail—contributes the most value to the final product .

Stage Ranking

Examine the production stages of olive oil and rank them from highest to lowest value contribution to learn why earlier stages often matter more in the supply chain.

Transparency Risk

Perform a deep-dive analysis on a coffee product to identify the biggest transparency risk by finding the stage where high value contribution meets weak evidence
